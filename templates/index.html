About Us

Lagos Transport Fest is the foremost gathering of the industryâ€™s finest minds, top-level business leaders and decision-makers within Road, Air and Water Transport sectors. At the conference & exhibition, industry experts, leaders & government authorities will share major insights concerning the Nigerian Transport market, as well as proffer solutions to these issues.

Lagos Transport Fest will provide an opportunity for learning, knowledge transfer, interaction and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Transport Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions ensuring a delegate audience of key decision-makers and c-suite directors.

Join 1000+ delegates and thought leaders, trend setters to refresh thinking, exchange ideas on the future of Transport and connect with other stakeholders & decision makers.


Why you should attend

Lagos Transport Fest will create an opportunity to:

  • -Explore business opportunities within the new mobility system.

  • - Network with policy-setting government figures at Federal and State Governments levels and benefit from collaboration and knowledge transfer.

  • - Share conversations with Policy makers as regards what they are doing to support innovation and investment.

  • - Discuss regulation of the use of personal data relating to transport activities.

  • - Learn on how transport is evolving with the rise of autonomy and connectivity.
